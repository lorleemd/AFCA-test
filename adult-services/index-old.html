Adult Services

Alaska Family Care welcomes adults of all ages

Services include:

  • Acute Care: upper respiratory infections, flu, lacerations, sprains/strains, joint injections etc…
  • Physical Exams: including gynecological exams for women, sports physicals, biometric screening, CDL/DOT exams
  • Travel Medicine: vaccinations, altitude and prophylactic medications
  • Chronic disease managment: hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, diabetes, obesity, mood disorders, athmas, allergies, etc…
  • Orthopedic services: including chronic management of arthritis and joint injections
  • Dermatology: including cryotherapy of benign lesion, excision or biospy of other lesions, laceration repair
  • Gynecological care: including placement of IUDs, contraceptive management
  • Weight Loss Management: non surgical counseling

If there is a medical issue outside of our scope of care, we have an outstanding network of specialists with whom we coordinate your care.
